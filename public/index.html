Settings

Email (SMTP) Configuration

Connect your business email to send outreach from the app. Contact your email host for SMTP details.

Common SMTP settings:
  • Gmail Workspace: smtp.gmail.com, port 587
  • Microsoft 365: smtp.office365.com, port 587
  • GoDaddy: smtpout.secureserver.net, port 465 (SSL)
  • cPanel hosts: mail.yourdomain.com, port 587

📥 IMAP / RoundCube Sync

Connect your IMAP inbox so sent emails appear in your RoundCube Sent folder, and replies from contacts sync back into the CRM Activity Log.

Usually "Sent" — check RoundCube for exact name

Uses the same username & password as SMTP above.

RoundCube / cPanel hosts (most common):
  • IMAP Host: mail.yourdomain.com, port 993, SSL
  • Sent folder: Sent (or INBOX.Sent for some hosts)
  • Username & password: same as your email login

✉ Email Signature
